# Flight triage guide

## High-priority rejection signals

Reject or strongly down-rank when any of these appear:

- self-transfer between separate tickets
- airport change inside a connection
- overnight layover for elderly travelers unless requested
- transit through an excluded country/region
- unclear transit legality for visa-sensitive travelers
- very short international layover (< 90 min) unless same airline and proven protected connection

## Positive signals

Prefer when these appear:

- one ticket end-to-end
- same airline or strong alliance interline
- connection 2-4 hours
- no airport/terminal complexity
- clear baggage through-check language
- route already common for the traveler profile

## Recommendation labels

### Strong

Use when the itinerary is legal-looking, simple, protected, and competitively priced.

### Okay

Use when the itinerary is workable but has a tradeoff such as longer travel time, higher price, or a connection that needs mild caution.

### Avoid

Use when the itinerary is risky, brittle, confusing, or likely mismatched to the traveler's constraints.
